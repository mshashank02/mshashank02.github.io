About Me

I am a second year M.S. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering) at University of California, Davis. My research interests include Control Systems, Reinforcement Learning, and Robotics.

I currently work with Prof. Iman Soltani on dexterous manipulation using tactile sensing on humanoid robotics arms. I also work with UC Davis EcoCar on implementing Cooperative Adaptive Cruise Control and Lane Centering Control on a Cadillac LYRIQ as part of the EcoCAR competition.
